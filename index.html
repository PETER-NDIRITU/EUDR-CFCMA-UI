Ensuring EUDR Compliance

Our Web-GIS application helps coffee farmers in Muringato Catchment maintain compliance with European Union Deforestation Regulations, ensuring continued access to the European market after 31st December 2025.

  • Real-time deforestation monitoring
  • Automated coffee farmers' compliance reporting
